A Brief Introduction

Hello, I am Isabella, and this is my bioSite. I am a Senior at Morgantown High School and aspire to attend college after taking a brief hiatus after graduating. I enjoy spending time with my friends at the mall, movie theater, and pool when I am not working. I also enjoy talking to and visiting with my step-brother and step-sister, who still resides in Iowa, but fly out as often as possible to visit. I enjoy reading, watching movies, listening to music, and playing video games when I have free time. I also enjoy astronomy, which I first became interested in when I was little and lived in Eldora, Iowa when my father bought a 10-inch Dobsonian telescope. We would spend hours looking at the planets, nebula, galaxies, and stars every night the sky was clear.

When I was ten, my parents divorced, and my father moved to West Virginia. I would visit him every chance I got, and when I was twelve, I moved to West Virginia to live with him. It was quite a shock moving from a small town where everyone knew each other. It took me a couple of years (partially due to having to homeschool during the COVID-19 pandemic) but I finally adjusted and now love where I live. I am still discovering myself and the things I love and enjoy daily, and I am becoming more excited for the future and what it may hold. After all, my journey is only beginning, and I intend it to be a cosmic adventure. I hope you enjoy discovering more about me on the pages of this bioSite and that your life's adventure is as cosmic as mine!
